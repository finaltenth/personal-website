Giant design system

How can we ensure all projects regardless of timescale have a solid technical foundation following UX best practices. And how can this be replicated as part of the visual design process.

The problem

How can we ensure all projects regardless of timescale have a solid technical foundation following UX best practices.

The process

With a limited timescale available for devleopment initial research and concepting was conducted with reference to existing industry best practice and similar design systems.

It was identified that Sketch libraries would be an ideal means of storing the visual elements of the toolkit.

The creation process

  • A series of rough scamps were created of all the key UI elements of the design system
  • Initial research based on industry best practice, and cross referenced with existing design systems such as BBC (GEL), Co-op and GDS guidelines.
  • Creation of bare bones interface library in Sketch.
  • Continual refinement of element based on real world usage.
  • All the elements were documented
  • Testing against adherance to WCAG AA compliance level
